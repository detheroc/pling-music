Superampify 2.0

Superampify is the Android client for the music streaming service in Runners-ID. We have done a careful integration of free and libre software web-services.

We currently offer a 5GB free space per account, and it's free to register. So, what are you waiting for? Get your free account now.

Superampify is based on the awesome subsonic android client by Sindre Mehus. Feel free to thank him donating on his project. We do, however, not encourage his ad and donationware version of the project, and thus, superampify is just a clean fork meeting the needs of Runners-ID.
