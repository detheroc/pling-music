Pling Music 0.99

Pling Music is the Android client for the music streaming service in Pling.it. Any music you own or like in Pling, will be available to you in Pling Music.

Pling Music is based on the awesome subsonic android client by Sindre Mehus. If you like this app, feel free to thank him donating to his project. Access our local subsonic's fork at Pling Music for Android@github
